## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are HR staff who need to export attendance and overtime data for a chosen date range. They use the interface as an internal operations tool where speed, clarity, and trust matter more than marketing language.

### Brand Personality
Modern, premium, controlled. The interface should feel reliable and precise, with a calm operational tone that reduces friction for repetitive export tasks.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a polished editorial-operations look rather than a generic SaaS dashboard. Support both light and dark modes. Favor crisp structure, tinted neutrals, restrained copper-gold accents, and strong hierarchy. Avoid playful visuals, noisy card grids, and template-looking startup gradients.

### Design Principles
- Make the primary export workflow obvious within seconds.
- Use hierarchy and spacing to reduce cognitive load during repetitive tasks.
- Signal accuracy, auditability, and operational confidence through the visual system.
- Keep interactions and copy concise, practical, and free of filler.
- Prefer adaptable layouts that still feel composed on narrow screens.
